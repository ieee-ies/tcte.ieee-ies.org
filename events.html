Events

  • ISIE 2024 - 33rd International Symposium on Industrial Electronics

    The TCTE is proud to manage Technical Track TT 01 - New Technologies for Electric Transportation at the upcoming ISIE 2024.

    This track will focus on the latest advancements in electric transportation technology, showcasing innovative research and applications. We invite researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to join us for insightful discussions and networking opportunities.

    For more information on the technical tracks and to register, please visit the ISIE 2024 Technical Tracks page.

  • PECTEA 2025 - 1st International Conference on Power Electronic Converters in Transportation and Energy Applications

    The TCTE is proud to manage Technical Track - Electric Mobility and Transportation at the upcoming PECTEA 2025.

    This track aims to cover the latest advancements, technologies, and systems related to electric power-trains, charging infrastructures, energy storage, power electronics, communication systems, and thermal management, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the current and future trends in electric transportation and energy applications. We invite researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to join us for insightful discussions and networking opportunities.

    For more information on the technical tracks and to register, please visit the PECTEA 2025 Technical Tracks page.
