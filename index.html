TC on Transportation Electrification

Welcome to the IEEE IES Technical Committee on Transportation Electrification (TCTE) Website!

Transportation electrification is transforming the automotive industry, reducing reliance on fossil fuels, and significantly lowering emissions. The IEEE IES TCTE is at the forefront, facilitating pivotal discussions and research in this rapidly evolving field.

About Us

This committee serves as a dynamic forum for researchers and practitioners to share their latest achievements, explore cutting-edge technologies, and address critical challenges in transportation electrification. Our activities range from organizing conferences and workshops to publishing groundbreaking research and fostering global collaborations.
